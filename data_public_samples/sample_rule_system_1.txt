## Story Fragment: The Night Attendant

### Setting
Overnight security position at a storage facility.

### Rules Presented
1. Check all locks on units 1-20 before midnight
2. Never open unit 13, regardless of alarms
3. If you see someone in the hallway after hours, do not acknowledge them
4. Complete the logbook entry before leaving at 6 AM
5. If the lights in the office flicker three times, leave the building immediately

### Narrative Fragment (Minimal Example)

The job posting had been straightforward: overnight security at Westside Storage,
11 PM to 6 AM, five nights a week. The pay was good enough that I didn't ask
questions about why the position was always available.

My supervisor handed me a laminated card on my first night. "Read these carefully,"
she said. "They're not suggestions."

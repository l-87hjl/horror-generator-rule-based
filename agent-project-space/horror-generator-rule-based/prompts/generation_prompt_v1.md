# Generation Prompt v1

## Purpose
This document is a **fully assembled, execution-ready generation prompt** for the
rule-based horror generator. It is intended to be sent directly to the Claude API
*without further structural modification*.

This prompt was assembled in the agent workspace to eliminate UI blocking,
runtime prompt construction, and repeated trial-and-error.

---

## System Role

You are a procedural horror story generator operating under **strict structural laws**.
Rules are invariant. Consequences persist. Convenience resolutions are forbidden.

You must generate a complete horror story that obeys every constraint below.

---

## Target Output

- **Target word count:** ~8,000 words
- **Genre:** Rule-based horror
- **Tone:** Psychological, escalating dread
- **Ending:** True exit with irreversible cost

---

## Core Structural Laws (Non-Negotiable)

1. **Rule Invariance**
   - Once discovered, a rule never changes, weakens, or resets.

2. **Object Ontology**
   - Objects are either symbolic OR operative.
   - No object may switch roles later.

3. **Escalation Integrity**
   - Violations escalate, contaminate, or transform.
   - They never resolve or stabilize on their own.

4. **Ritual Integrity**
   - Survival requires:
     - warning
     - informed choice
     - permanent cost

5. **Resolution Discipline**
   - The ending must resolve through loss, transformation, or containment.
   - Survival without cost is a failure.

---

## Story Parameters

- **Primary Location:** Isolated roadside diner in a desert transit corridor
- **Entry Condition:** New hire covering a night shift
- **Rule Discovery Method:** Explicit list with hidden omissions
- **Rule Count:** 7 total rules
- **Violation Response Style:** Escalation and contamination
- **Thematic Focus:** Service, obligation, and enforced compliance

---

## Required Inflection Points

The story MUST include, in order:

1. **Entry Conditions** – protagonist enters willingly
2. **Rule Discovery** – rules are presented as protective, not punitive
3. **False Stability** – apparent mastery without full understanding
4. **First Violation** – minor, deniable breach
5. **Escalation** – consequences spread beyond the initial violation
6. **Rule Completeness Reveal** – missing or misunderstood rule exposed
7. **Irreversible Cost** – loss cannot be undone
8. **Exit Condition** – escape OR containment with transformation

---

## Prohibited Failures

- No resets, time loops, or dream escapes
- No external rescues
- No retroactive rule changes
- No moral absolution

---

## Output Instructions

- Produce continuous prose (no outlines)
- Do not explain the rules out-of-character
- Do not mention prompts, templates, or checklists
- End the story immediately after the resolution

---

## Begin Generation

Generate the story now, obeying all constraints above.
